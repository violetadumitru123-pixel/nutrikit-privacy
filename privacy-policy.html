NutriKit Pro

Privacy Policy - Last updated: June 2026

1. Data Collection

NutriKit Pro collects and stores data locally on your device: personal information (name, age, weight, height, gender), nutritional data (food logs, calorie intake, macros), app preferences, and weight history.

2. Data Storage

All nutritional data is stored locally on your device. We use Supabase for authentication only (email and encrypted password). Your nutrition data never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

USDA FoodData Central API: Food search queries, no personal data sent.
Open Food Facts API: Barcode lookups, no personal data sent.
Supabase: Authentication only.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties.

5. Data Deletion

Delete all data anytime via Delete Account in Profile. This permanently removes all local data and your authentication record.

6. Notifications

Meal reminders are scheduled locally. No data is sent to external servers.

7. Data Sources

USDA FoodData Central - Food database
Open Food Facts - Barcode database
Mifflin-St Jeor Equation - Calorie estimation

8. Children

Not intended for children under 13.
